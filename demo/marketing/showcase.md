# built.growthx.club showcase blurb

**Career Computa** (Track 3: AI as Agency)

A career agency run by agents, with a real job seeker as client zero. Sign up with your own LinkedIn export or resume (voice onboarding included) and the crew takes over the volume work of a live job search: a manager agent plans each task and delegates to specialists that poll real company job boards (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby), score fit against your actual resume with the caveats spelled out, research the company via live search with every claim linked to a source, tailor a resume variant through six quality gates, and write your LinkedIn connection note and DM draft. The finished package lands in your Telegram; your only job is the send decision inside LinkedIn.

The signature mechanic is trust graduation: every draft type carries a trust meter, a streak of clean approvals turns the review tap off for that type, one edit resets it, one click revokes it. Watch the tap disappear as the agent earns it.

Everything is verifiable live: a public ledger lists every completed task with a verify link to the live posting, the full multi-agent trace with per-step cost, and the artifacts. Failures stay visible. Built on-site in the 8-hour sprint with Hermes as coding partner and runtime, Convex as the backend, Cloudflare hosting, Linkup research, and Wispr voice input.

Live ledger: https://career-agency-web.bsplaza.workers.dev
